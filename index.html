Hello! Afforder is a little app to help you budget. It's very simple and all your data stays on your phone. If you have any questions or feedback, please email iamsteve@gmail.com.

Concept
Provide Afforder with your monthly spending budget and it will divide that into a daily budget. Try and stay within that budget. If you spend less, your daily budget will go up throughout the month. If you spend more, you'll see a warning showing how much you've reduced your budget by. Afforder assumes you've already figured out your bills, savings, etc.

Installing on iOS
Tap the share button, scroll down, tap "Add to home screen".

Installing on Android
Open the menu, tap "Add to home screen".

Usage
Tap on the monthly budget to set it.
Tap on your daily budget (top of the app) to record spending.
Tap on items in your spend history to delete them.

Notes
Afforder is a PWA (progressive web app) that works offline and uses your phone's cache to store data. Most phones will persist this data but there's no guarantee.

This is an experiment. If it turns out to be useful, I'll make this into a fully-fledged iOS/Android app. Please do let me know if it's been helpful, or if you have any ideas.
